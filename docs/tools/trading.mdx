<Warning>
Every tool on this page is **guarded**: it requires `PEAR_READ_ONLY=false` **and** `confirm: true` on the call. Without both, it returns an error and does nothing. See [Safety](/get-started/safety).
</Warning>

## Shared shapes

**`pairAsset`** (used in `longAssets` / `shortAssets`):

<ParamField path="asset" type="string" required>Asset symbol, e.g. `BTC`, `ETH`, `NEAR`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="weight" type="number">Allocation 0.0001–1.0; omitted = even split.</ParamField>

**`tpSl`** (take-profit / stop-loss):

<ParamField path="type" type="enum" required>`PERCENTAGE` · `DOLLAR` · `POSITION_VALUE` · `PRICE` · `PRICE_RATIO` · `WEIGHTED_RATIO`. `WEIGHTED_RATIO` works for baskets.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="value" type="number">Threshold value.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="isTrailing" type="boolean">Enable trailing.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="trailingDeltaValue" type="number">Trailing distance.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="trailingActivationValue" type="number">Price/level at which trailing activates.</ParamField>

---

## `pear_open_position`

Open a pair or basket trade. `longAssets`/`shortAssets` accept multiple weighted legs, managed afterward as a single consolidated position.

<ParamField path="usdValue" type="number" required>Position size in USD (≥1).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="leverage" type="number" default="1">1–100.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="slippage" type="number" default="0.01">0.001–0.1 (`0.01` = 1%).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="executionType" type="enum" default="MARKET">`SYNC` · `MARKET` · `TRIGGER` · `TWAP` · `LADDER`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="longAssets" type="pairAsset[]" default="[]">Long legs.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="shortAssets" type="pairAsset[]" default="[]">Short legs.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="triggerType" type="enum">`PRICE` · `PRICE_LIMIT` · `PRICE_RATIO` · `WEIGHTED_RATIO` · `BTC_DOM` · `CROSS_ASSET_PRICE` · `PREDICTION_MARKET_OUTCOME`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="triggerValue" type="string">Trigger threshold.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="direction" type="enum">`MORE_THAN` · `LESS_THAN`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="twapDuration" type="number">Minutes (TWAP only).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="twapIntervalSeconds" type="number">1–3600.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="stopLoss" type="tpSl" />
<ParamField path="takeProfit" type="tpSl" />
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required>Must be `true` to execute.</ParamField>

At least one leg across `longAssets`/`shortAssets` is required. `POST /positions`.

---

## `pear_set_risk`

Set or update take-profit and stop-loss on a position. Pass `null` to remove either.

<ParamField path="positionId" type="string" required />
<ParamField path="stopLoss" type="tpSl | null" />
<ParamField path="takeProfit" type="tpSl | null" />
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`PUT /positions/{positionId}/riskParameters` with `{ stopLoss, takeProfit }` (missing → `null`).

---

## `pear_adjust_position`

Increase or reduce a position by a percentage.

<ParamField path="positionId" type="string" required />
<ParamField path="adjustmentType" type="enum" default="REDUCE">`REDUCE` · `INCREASE`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="adjustmentSize" type="number" required>1–100 (%).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="executionType" type="enum" default="MARKET">`MARKET` · `LIMIT`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="limitRatio" type="number">Required if `executionType=LIMIT`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`POST /positions/{positionId}/adjust`.

---

## `pear_rebalance_execute`

Rebalance a position's legs to target weights — **executes trades**. Preview first with [`pear_rebalance_plan`](/tools/rebalance#pear-rebalance-plan).

<ParamField path="positionId" type="string" required />
<ParamField path="targetWeights" type="object">Omit to use stored targets.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`POST /positions/{positionId}/rebalance`.

---

## `pear_close_position`

Close a single position.

<ParamField path="positionId" type="string" required />
<ParamField path="executionType" type="enum" default="MARKET">`MARKET` · `TWAP` · `TRIGGER`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="twapDuration" type="number">Minutes (TWAP).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="triggerType" type="enum">`PRICE` · `PRICE_RATIO` · `WEIGHTED_RATIO` · `PERCENTAGE` · `DOLLAR` · `POSITION_VALUE`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="triggerValue" type="string" />
<ParamField path="direction" type="enum">`MORE_THAN` · `LESS_THAN`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`POST /positions/{positionId}/close`.

---

## `pear_close_all_positions`

Close every open position sequentially.

<ParamField path="executionType" type="enum" default="MARKET">`MARKET` · `TWAP`.</ParamField>
<ParamField path="twapDuration" type="number">Minutes (TWAP).</ParamField>
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`POST /positions/close-all`.

---

## `pear_cancel_order`

Cancel an open order by id.

<ParamField path="orderId" type="string" required />
<ParamField path="confirm" type="boolean" default="false" required />

`DELETE /orders/{orderId}/cancel`.
